Let Us Tell You Our Story

Srigandha/Chandana or sandal wood is synonymous with Kannada and Karnataka. There is a view among scholars that the word Kannada is derived from kammittu+nadu, that is ‘fragrant land’, as the land where Kannada is spoken is full of fragrance of sandal wood trees which are common in Karnataka.

About Karnataka

Royal tree of Karnataka

Since ancient times Karnataka was the largest grower of sandal wood trees. The Chinese traveler, Hiuen Tsang refers to the sandal trees growing in Malayagiri (Malaya mountains). Eminent Sanskrit poet and critic Rajashekara mentions Malaya as the home of Chandana. Panchatantra states that Chandana grows nowhere else except on Malaya mountains. Scholars have identified this Malayagiri with the southern portion of the western ghats running from the south of Mysore and forming the eastern boundary of Travancore State. So famous was Karnataka for its sandal trees that Amarasimha in his lexicon Amarakosha mentions Malayaja (product of the Malaya mountain) as one among the four synonymous for sandal wood.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a person from non agricultural background buy agricultural land?

As per Karnataka land reform act of 2015, anyone can purchase agriculture land in Karnataka

Is it legal to grow sandalwood trees privately?

In 2015, the Karnataka legislative assembly passed the much-awaited legislation to deregulate the sandalwood business, by lifting government monopoly over sandalwood. The Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Bill, 2001 will now allow cultivation of sandal trees in private lands and the government will also revise its cost.

Why are you charging additional maintenance cost?

Sandalwood is a high value asset which should be protected and nourished to reap the benefit. We will invest in state of the art security and irrigation system which requires additional cost to maintain. The maintain cost includes – Guards, Electricity bills, Organic Manure, Agriculture labors, equipment maintenance, borewell maintenance.

Why you have selected Hiriyur for your project?

We have price advantage with respective to land and labor and we have ample amount of water source from ground water and vedhavathi river.

How far it is from Bangalore?

Its around 180 Kms from Bangalore center and around 80 Kms from Tumkur

Can i sell the property whenever I want?

Yes you can sell the property only after 5 years, we have buy back option or you can sell in open market.

Why should I wait for 15 years to reap the benefit, can I cut the trees before 15 years?

The value of sandalwood is so high due to its long duration of maturity, it takes minimum 8 year for a sandalwood to form the core.

Who are sandalwood buyers and consumers?

The major consumer is Karnataka Soaps and Detergents Limited (KSDL), a company owned by the government of Karnataka and second major consumer is Karnataka State Handicrafts Development Corporation Limited, (A Government of Karnataka Undertaking)

Can I take bank loan to purchase property in your project?

Banks are not providing loan to purchase agriculture land, however we have very attractive only 10% p.a EMI scheme which you can avail from us using ESC mode.

Why you have chosen sandalwood?

Sandalwood has global and regional market. It is used for multi purpose like – fragrance , medicine, woodcraft

How do you secure the trees from theft?

We will use high tech security systems like solar fencing, CCTV, motion and thermal detection and conventional security system of Guards and trained watch dogs.

Why not plant only sandalwood? Why are you adding neem trees?

Sandalwood depends on host plant for its nutrition requirement, neem is one of the good nutrition provider for sandalwood.
